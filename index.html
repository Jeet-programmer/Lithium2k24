Register

For entry to the fest cultural events, the day pass is necessary. A QR code will be generated which you will be required to show at the gate on entrance and will be given a band.

Please ensure the size of your team is not more than the maximum given by the event rules. For team events such as Groove, MasterShot, MECHacks, etc, only one person will be able to register per team. Under the attendee name slot, please enter your teammates names as well.

College ID is compulsory for entry at the gate.

MEC students are not required to take day passes nor do they have to register for MECHacks and Groove (They do however need to register for every other event that requires a fee)
